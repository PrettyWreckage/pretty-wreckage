Where reason and desire collide,  
A battlefield of hope and doubt,  
Where silence screams and tears pour out.  

Mind builds walls to keep me safe,  
Protecting from a bitter fate,  
But heart breaks through with tender fire,  
Fueled by dreams and deep desire.  

I’m caught in this relentless dance,  
Between the fear and second chance,  
Wrestling logic, loving blind,  
Trying to untangle what’s entwined.  

And though the road ahead is tough,  
And sometimes love feels not enough,  
I choose to stay, to fight, to feel,  
Because the heart’s wounds also heal.  

Mind may say run, but I will stay,  
Holding love in my own way,  
For sometimes courage is to remain,  
To love despite the ache and pain.
