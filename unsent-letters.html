Unsent Letters

To the one I still dream about
I wrote this a hundred times in my head, but none of the versions felt enough. Maybe this one won’t either, but it’s the only one I had the courage to almost send.
Dear You, who taught me goodbye
I never said thank you for the lesson. I never forgave you either. But I still write to you in my quietest moments, and wonder if any part of you ever whispers my name.
