Unsent Letters

These letters were never sent—but they carry every word I wish I could say. Each one was written with trembling hands, in quiet moments, and hidden tears.

Some are meant for people who will never read them. Others are meant for the version of me I used to be.
Thank you for opening them gently. ❤️

Unsent Letter #1
I hope you're doing okay. I keep rewriting this letter hoping the words will land softer than they used to. Maybe one day, it won’t ache like this. But today it still does.
Unsent Letter #2
I wanted to say I’m sorry. For the things I said, and for the things I didn’t. For how I held on too long—and also for letting go the way I did.
